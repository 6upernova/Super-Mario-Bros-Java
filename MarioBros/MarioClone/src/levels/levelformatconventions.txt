Levels format: 

Level name convention: levelX.txt
        X represents the number of level

int type, int position in x, int position in y

type is represented by int number:
    type        vulues  represents
            1       character    
    enemies

            3       KoopaTroopa
            4       Piranha Plant
            5       Lakitu
            6       Spiny
            7       Buzzy Beetle
            8       Shell

    power ups
            10      Mushroom
            11      FireFlower
            12      Star
            13      GreenMushroom
            14      Coin
    platforms   
            20      SolidBlock
            21      BickBlock
            22      QuestionBlock
            23      Pipe
            24      Void
    projetiles
            30      Fireball
            31      SpinyEgg
